Ladys & Moonscape

สำนักพิมพ์ลาดิดและมูนสเคป

Be Queer, Be Protagonist is our motto.

With the collaboration between two writers, our desire is to broaden the scope of the Thai literature industry. We strive to produce books where characters from LGBTQAI+ community come to life as the main characters without being limited in any particular genre.

At present, our small publisher has produced more than ten books. You can buy Ladys and Moonscape Books at participating independent bookstores, all Kinokuniya outlets, and the home page displayed on this website.
