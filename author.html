Ladys

WRITER
Lady’s Lady | be attributed to V. W. | Bread & Roses

 LADYS was born in 1997. She graduated with a bachelor’s degree in medicine, but never became a medical doctor. LADYS is now a freelance writer who made her name known with the first novel, Beau Is Non-Binary of Everything (2021), followed by further books, amongst which are Stream of Concetta (2021), and Ms. Kent & Me (2022). Owing to their belief in the fluidity of the human genders, their aspiration to create a fictional world that embraces all differences, and with LADYS herself being non-binary (she/they), the characters in the works are rich in diversity, be it their sexuality or ethnicity. Furthermore, LADYS is fascinated with stream-of-consciousness writing, seeing it as a nature of thoughts.

 LADYS lives in Phetchaburi, Thailand—a province with delicious food, beaches, and mountain ranges. She can be found on Twitter and on Instagram under the same handle as @TheoryofLady.
